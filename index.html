About the Troop

We are a troop made up of many scouts and have awarded many Eagle scouts.

Why should I join Troop?

  • Monthly Camping trips
  • Weekly Troop Meetings
  • Recognition for advancement in the troop

You must be 11 years old to join a boy scout troop. There are many scouts all over the world, and it is a very prestigious, significant, and fun-filled program to be part of. If you live near Hasbrouck Heights New Jersey and interested in joining a boy scout troop, feel free to contact us!

Boy Scout Activities

Boy Scouts take part in many fun activities throughout the year. When you become a boy scout you could go camping, work on merit badges, and achieve rank requirements.

Camping

When joining Boy Scout Troop 17 you have the opportunity to go camping with other scouts, involving many activities such as hiking, visiting places, rifle shooting, kayaking/canoeing, ziplining, and more!

Advancement

In Boy Scouts there are ranks, awards, and merit badges you can earn. In Troop 17 we will help you understand these achievements and help you earn them in order to be a successful scout.

Merit Badges

Merit badges are awards given to scouts who complete an area of study. There are more than 135 merit badges available for scouts to earn. These merit badges give you scouting skills, life skills, and other skills.

Community Service

As being part of Troop 17, we actively aim to help our community. Examples include supporting the local food pantry, helping local schools, helping religious institutions, and restoring the local VFW.

Learning Essential Skills

Essential skills are very important, and scouting can help you earn them. These skills include: character, leadership, citizenship, fitness, first aid, aquatics, learning about the nature, outdoor ethics, hiking, camping, cooking, navigation, working with tools, and personal safety awareness.

Hiking

Troop 17 goes on hikes during camping trips and also does hikes on weekends as a troop. Usually hikes are 4-20 miles long and on camping trips scouts cook a meal to bring with them on the hike.

Known Around the World

Boy Scouts is the largest youth organization in the United States, awarding Eagle Scouts such as Neil Armstrong, Michael Mauler (CEO of GameStop), Sam Walton (Founder of Walmart), Steven Spielberg (Director), Michael Bloomberg (Founder of Bloomberg L.P.), William Hanna (Director of Flintstones), and Lawrence Bacow (President of Harvard).

Frequently Asked Questions

Some frequently asked questions about Scouting and Troop 17.

  • How much money does scouting cost?

    A BSA membership fee is around $33 per scouting year and changes yearly. Individual camping trip fees aren’t included with the yearly fee.

  • Where does my son have to live to join Troop 17?

    Your son has to live in Bergen County, and preferably in Hasbrouck Heights.

  • How often do you participate in camping and other activities?

    Camping is usually once a month, volunteering and other activities vary by the different opportunities.
